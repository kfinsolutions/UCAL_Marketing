AIR SUCTION VALVE

Salient Features

  • Compact size & less weight
  • Flexible mounting with vehicle
  • Snap fit design for ease of manufacturing and low cost
  • Ease of assemble with vehicle
  • Prompt response for ON/OFF
  • Low operating noise
  • Low Current drawn
  • Wide operating voltage range
  • Water proof design
  • Can withstand vibration up to 20g
  • High durable with cost efficient design
  • E-ASV is a device, which helps to reduce the pollutants like Carbon monoxide (CO) and Hydrocarbons (HC) in the exhaust gas by post oxidation in exhaust
  • Facilitate early catalytic converter light-off & speeds up the onset of Lambda control action.
  • The solenoid will control the air flow and supply the air to exhaust system based on the Electronic Control Unit input
  • Operate in closed-loop system.
  • Flow rate: ≥ 70 Liters /Min at 300 mm of H2O Pressure.

Application

  • Exhaust emission control part (EASV) for 2 wheelers
